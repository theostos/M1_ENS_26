    Links between independence and characteristic function #

    Two random variables are independent if and only if their joint characteristic function is equal to the product of the characteristic functions. More specifically, prove this in Hilbert spaces for two variables and a finite family of variables. We prove the analogous statements in Banach spaces, with an arbitrary Lp norm, for the dual characteristic function.
