    Projective resolution of k as a trivial k-linear representation of a finite cyclic group #

    Let k be a commutative ring and G a finite commutative group. Given g : G and A : Rep k G, we can define a periodic chain complex in Rep k G given by ... ‚ü∂ A --N--> A --(œÅ(g) - ùüô)--> A --N--> A --(œÅ(g) - ùüô)--> A ‚ü∂ 0 where N is the norm map sending a : A to ‚àë œÅ(g)(a) for all g in G.
