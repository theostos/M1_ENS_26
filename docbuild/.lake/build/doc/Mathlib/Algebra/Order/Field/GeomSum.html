    Partial sums of geometric series in an ordered field #

    This file upper- and lower-bounds the values of the geometric series $\sum_{i=0}^{n-1} x^i$ and $\sum_{i=0}^{n-1} x^i y^{n-1-i}$ and variants thereof.
