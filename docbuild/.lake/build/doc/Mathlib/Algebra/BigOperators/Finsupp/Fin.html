    noncomputable def finTwoArrowEquiv' (M : Type u_1) [Zero M] :
    (Fin 2 →₀ M) ≃ M × M

    The space of finitely supported functions Fin 2 →₀ α is equivalent to α × α. See also finTwoArrowEquiv.
