    Manifold structure on real intervals #

    The manifold structure on real intervals is defined in Mathlib.Geometry.Manifold.Instances.Real. We relate it to the manifold structure on the real line, by showing that the inclusion (contMDiff_subtype_coe_Icc) and projection (contMDiffOn_projIcc) are smooth, and showing that a function defined on the interval is smooth iff its composition with the projection is smooth on the interval in ‚Ñù (see contMDiffOn_comp_projIcc_iff and friends).

    We also define 1 : TangentSpace (ùì°‚àÇ 1) z, and relate it to 1 in the real line.
