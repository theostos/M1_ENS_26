    (J.sheafify P).comp F ≅ J.sheafify (P.comp F)

    The isomorphism between the sheafification of P composed with F and the sheafification of P ⋙ F.

    Use the lemmas whisker_right_to_sheafify_sheafify_comp_iso_hom, to_sheafify_comp_sheafify_comp_iso_inv and sheafify_comp_iso_inv_eq_sheafify_lift to reduce the components of this isomorphism to a state that can be handled using the universal property of sheafification.
