    Balanced categories and functors reflecting isomorphisms #

    If a category is C, and a functor out of C reflects epimorphisms and monomorphisms, then the functor reflects isomorphisms. Furthermore, categories that admits a functor that ReflectsIsomorphisms, PreservesEpimorphisms and PreservesMonomorphisms are balanced.
