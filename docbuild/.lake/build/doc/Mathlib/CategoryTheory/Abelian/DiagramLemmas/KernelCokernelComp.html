    Long exact sequence for the kernel and cokernel of a composition #

    If f : X ‚ü∂ Y and g : Y ‚ü∂ Z are composable morphisms in an abelian category, we construct a long exact sequence : 0 ‚ü∂ ker f ‚ü∂ ker (f ‚â´ g) ‚ü∂ ker g ‚ü∂ coker f ‚ü∂ coker (f ‚â´ g) ‚ü∂ coker g ‚ü∂ 0.

    This is obtained by applying the snake lemma to the following morphism of exact sequences, where the rows are the obvious split exact sequences

    0 ‚ü∂ X ‚ü∂ X ‚äû Y ‚ü∂ Y ‚ü∂ 0
    |f    |œÜ    |g
    v     v     v
0 ‚ü∂ Y ‚ü∂ Y ‚äû Z ‚ü∂ Z ‚ü∂ 0

    and œÜ is given by the following matrix:

    (f  -ùüô Y)
(0     g)

    Indeed the snake lemma gives an exact sequence involving the kernels and cokernels of the vertical maps: in order to get the expected long exact sequence, it suffices to obtain isomorphisms ker œÜ ‚âÖ ker (f ‚â´ g) and coker œÜ ‚âÖ coker (f ‚ãô g).
