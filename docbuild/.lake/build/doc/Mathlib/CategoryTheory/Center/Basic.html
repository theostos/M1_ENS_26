    The center of a category #

    Given a category C, we introduce an abbreviation CatCenter C for the center of the category C, which is End (ùü≠ C), the type of endomorphisms of the identity functor of C.

    References #

    • https://ncatlab.org/nlab/show/center+of+a+category

    abbrev CategoryTheory.CatCenter (C : Type u) [Category.{v, u} C] :
    Type (max u v)

    The center of a category C is the type End (ùü≠ C) of the endomorphisms of the identify functor of C.
