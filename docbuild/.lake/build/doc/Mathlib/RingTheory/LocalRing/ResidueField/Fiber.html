    The fiber of a ring homomorphism at a prime ideal #

    Main results #

    • Ideal.Fiber: p.Fiber S is the fiber of a prime p of R in an R-algebra S, defined to be κ(p) ⊗ S.
    • PrimeSpectrum.preimageHomeomorphFiber : We show that there is a homeomorphism between the fiber of the induced map PrimeSpectrum S → PrimeSpectrum R at a prime ideal p and the prime spectrum of p.Fiber S.

    The fiber of a prime p of R in an R-algebra S, defined to be κ(p) ⊗ S.

    See PrimeSpectrum.preimageHomeomorphFiber for the homeomorphism between the spectrum of it and the actual set-theoretic fiber of PrimeSpectrum S → PrimeSpectrum R at p.
