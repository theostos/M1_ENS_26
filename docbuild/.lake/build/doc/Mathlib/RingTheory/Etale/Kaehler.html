    Main results #

    • KaehlerDifferential.tensorKaehlerEquivOfFormallyEtale: The canonical isomorphism T ⊗[S] Ω[S⁄R] ≃ₗ[T] Ω[T⁄R] for T a formally étale S-algebra.
    • Algebra.tensorH1CotangentOfIsLocalization: The canonical isomorphism T ⊗[S] H¹(L_{S⁄R}) ≃ₗ[T] H¹(L_{T⁄R}) for T a localization of S.
