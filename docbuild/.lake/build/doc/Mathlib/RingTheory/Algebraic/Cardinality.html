    theorem Algebra.IsAlgebraic.cardinalMk_le_max (R : Type u) [CommRing R] (L : Type u) [CommRing L] [IsDomain L] [Algebra R L] [NoZeroSMulDivisors R L] [Algebra.IsAlgebraic R L] :

    The cardinality of an algebraic extension is at most the maximum of the cardinality of the base ring or ℵ₀.
