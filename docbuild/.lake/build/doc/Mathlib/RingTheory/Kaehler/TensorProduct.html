    Main results #

    • KaehlerDifferential.tensorKaehlerEquiv: (S ⊗[R] Ω[A⁄R]) ≃ₗ[S] Ω[B⁄S] for B = S ⊗[R] A.
    • KaehlerDifferential.isLocalizedModule_of_isLocalizedModule: Ω[Aₚ/Rₚ] is the localization of Ω[A/R] at p.
