    Modules of finite length #

    We define modules of finite length (IsFiniteLength) to be finite iterated extensions of simple modules, and show that a module is of finite length iff it is both Noetherian and Artinian, iff it admits a composition series.

    We do not make IsFiniteLength a class, instead we use [IsNoetherian R M] [IsArtinian R M].
