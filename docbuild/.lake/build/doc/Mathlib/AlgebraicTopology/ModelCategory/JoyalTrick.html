    A trick by Joyal #

    In order to construct a model category, we may sometimes have basically proven all the axioms with the exception of the left lifting property of cofibrations with respect to trivial fibrations. A trick by Joyal allows to obtain this lifting property under suitable assumptions, namely that cofibrations are stable under composition and cobase change. (The dual result is also formalized.)
