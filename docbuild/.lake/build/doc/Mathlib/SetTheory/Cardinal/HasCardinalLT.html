    The property of being of cardinality less than a cardinal #

    Given X : Type u and κ : Cardinal.{v}, we introduce a predicate HasCardinalLT X κ expressing that Cardinal.lift.{v} (Cardinal.mk X) < Cardinal.lift κ.
